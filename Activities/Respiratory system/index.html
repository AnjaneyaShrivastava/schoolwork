Human Body

A healthy body leads a healthy mind

Human Body is a wonderful machine with many parts. Each part has a special job to do. It can do many things. It can see, feel, move, play, grow, breathe and reproduce. An oraganism or a living being is made up of diffrent organ system, each performing diffrent activities.

Organ System

Diffrent organ system work together to carry out all major functions of the body.

  1. Skeleton System
  2. Muscular System
  3. Digestive System
  4. Respiratory System
  5. Circulatory System
  6. Nervous System
  7. Excretory System

We Should take care of all our organ system to keep fit and healthy

  • We should exercise regularly and eat healthy food.
  • We should do breathing exercises in the morning.
  • We should drink lots of water to flush out all wastes of the body.
  • We should take proper rest and sleep and do yogasanas.
